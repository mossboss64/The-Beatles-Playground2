A Brief History of the Beatles

Click a year to learn more!

1957

John Lennon forms The Quarrymen. Paul soon joins, then George Harrison.

1962

Ringo Starr arrives, completing the legendary lineup.

1963-64

Beatlemania rises with nonstop tours and hit singles.

1967

Sgt. Pepper redefines psychedelic and experimental music.

1969

Abbey Road features iconic songwriting and the famous crosswalk cover.

1970

The breakup ends an era as solo careers begin.
